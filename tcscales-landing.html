From the Founder

Why I Built TC Scales

"Most healthcare practices are sitting on a goldmine — their own patient list. The problem isn't traffic. It's that nobody taught them how to use what they already have."

I started TC Scales in 2025 to fix that. My first client was a doctor-led weight loss practice with six locations. They had thousands of email and SMS contacts and had never used them strategically. We built a Klaviyo system from zero — educational sequences on insulin resistance, cortisol, gut health — content that taught before it sold.

Four months later, that system had generated $24,800 in revenue with no paid ads, no new traffic, and no gimmicks. Just smart automation talking to people who already trusted the doctor.

TC Scales is deliberately small. I take on a limited number of clients and I'm personally involved in every campaign. You won't be handed off to a junior account manager six months in. When you work with TC Scales, you work with me — directly.

My Goal

To build the agency that healthcare and wellness practices think of first when they're ready to treat their marketing like a real investment — and to prove it works before asking for a long-term commitment.

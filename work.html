The Breakdown

Why These Numbers Hit Different

56.8%

Open Rate — Not a Typo

Industry average is 20–25%. We hit 56.8% by writing every email like it came from a person, not a brand. Education-first messaging and authority positioning made the doctor's emails something patients actually looked forward to.

7×

SMS Performance

A 13.7% SMS click rate — up to 7× the industry benchmark — because we used SMS correctly: short, personal, high-intent only. No spam, no noise. Just perfectly timed messages that feel like a direct text from the doctor.

$0

Ad Spend Required

Every dollar of revenue came from the existing list. 100 warm organic leads were already coming in every month — they just had no system to receive them. We built that system. The money was already there.

∞

Flows That Never Stop

The $3,615 in flow revenue isn't a one-time number — it compounds. Every new lead enters the same automated sequence and goes through the same journey. It earns while the doctor sees patients.

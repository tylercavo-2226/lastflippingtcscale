About TC Scales

Systems Brands Rely On

Founded in 2023, TC Scales is an elite Meta advertising agency focused on one thing: building paid social systems that scale predictably and profitably.

Our Mission

Precision Over Promises

Most agencies sell hope. We sell systems. Every campaign we build is grounded in data, tested rigorously, and scaled only when the numbers prove it.

We've managed over $10M in ad spend across medical practices, DTC brands, SaaS companies, and local service businesses — and our clients stay because results compound over time.

TC Scales was built for ambitious brands that treat advertising as an investment, not an expense.

Why We Exist

The digital advertising landscape is full of agencies that over-promise and under-deliver. We built TC Scales to be the agency we wished existed when we were on the client side — one that combines creative excellence with analytical rigor and communicates with complete transparency.

How We Work

Our 4-Step Growth System

1

Discovery & Strategy

We audit your existing funnel, understand your ideal customer, map your competitive landscape, and build a campaign blueprint tailored to your growth targets.

2

3

Optimize & Scale

Winners get budget. Losers get cut. We continuously iterate — new creatives, refined audiences, improved landing pages — until we hit your target ROAS or CPL.

Report & Retain

Weekly performance reports, monthly strategy reviews, and proactive communication keep you fully informed. Most clients stay for 12+ months because results compound.
